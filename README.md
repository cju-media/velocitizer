## Velocitizer - The Simple MIDI Velocity Randomizer
<img width="450" height="273" alt="Controls" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1492ff00-8592-49f3-9cff-6bf4a9e3b2e2" />

Randomize your velocity with one simple control. 

* Random Range: Randomizes your velocity within that percent of the recieved value. For example, if you sequence 80 velocity at 10% random range, the randomizer will generate vaules between 72-88.
